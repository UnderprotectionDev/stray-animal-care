# 01 — Overview

---

## 1. Executive Summary

### 1.1 Project Description

Paws of Hope is a donation-focused website for an individual animal lover who personally cares for stray cats and dogs in Turkey (Hatay & Malatya). Following the 2023 earthquake, ~100 cats and dogs are being fed, treated, and rehabilitated daily. The site will be built to collect donations, raise awareness, and ensure transparency in order to sustain these efforts.

### 1.2 Mission

"To ensure that animals struggling to survive on the streets can access their basic needs, to facilitate their access to nutrition and healthcare, and to raise awareness about animal welfare in society. These efforts, which began as individual initiatives, aim to grow into a broader solidarity network over time through the contributions of volunteers and supporters."

### 1.3 Vision

"To establish a sustainable support network aimed at improving the living conditions of stray animals across Turkey in the long term, and to continue these efforts under an institutional structure with a strong community of volunteers. The goal is to carry out more systematic, transparent, and effective work through an association (dernek) planned to be established in the future."

### 1.4 Values

- **Transparency:** All donations and expenditures are reported openly
- **Compassion and Respect:** Equal care is shown to every living being
- **Sustainability:** Long-term, systematic efforts are targeted
- **Community Solidarity:** Individual efforts grow through community strength
